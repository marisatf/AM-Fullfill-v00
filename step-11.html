11.2. How to Bookmark in Google Chrome

Follow these simple steps to bookmark the documents in Google Chrome for easy access:

  1. Navigate to the page you want to bookmark (e.g., Order Information Sheet or Order Issues Sheet).
  2. Click on the star icon located in the URL bar at the top of Chrome.
  3. In the drop-down menu that appears, you can edit the name of the bookmark (if necessary) and choose the folder where you want to save it.
  4. Click "Done" to save the bookmark.

Tip: You can also press Ctrl + D (Cmd + D on Mac) to open the bookmark menu directly.

Once saved, you can quickly access these links directly from your bookmarks bar or from the Chrome bookmarks menu.
