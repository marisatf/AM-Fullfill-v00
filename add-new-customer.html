Customer Onboarding - 12 Step Process

Your guide to onboarding a new customer

Download SOP
  • Step 1: Initial Access Setup – Set up initial access for the client and internal teams.
  • Step 2: Create Client Store Ticker – Create the unique store ticker for the client.
  • Step 3: Notify Fulfill Team – Notify the team about the new onboarding and provide necessary details.
  • Step 4: Introduce Team to Client – Introduce internal team members in the WhatsApp group.
  • Step 5: Internal Kickoff – Tag assigned team members and inform them of the client details.
  • Step 6: Start Fulfillment – Request the first order from the client and begin purchasing products.
  • Step 7: Share Operating Hours – Share China office hours with the client, considering their time zone.
  • Step 8: Set Up the Order Issues Sheet – Share the Order Issues Sheet and inform the client about its purpose.
  • Step 9: Confirm and Finalize Setup – Send the final sheet to the client and confirm receipt and understanding.
  • Step 10: Inform Internal Team – Notify the internal team in WeChat about the new Order Issues Sheet.
  • Step 11: Bookmark Key Documents – Bookmark the client’s Order Information and Order Issues Sheets on your computer for easy access.
  • Step 12: Schedule Weekly Calls – Schedule weekly calls with the client, set time zone, and log tasks in Trello for visibility.
