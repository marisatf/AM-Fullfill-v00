Step 12 - Schedule Weekly Calls

12.1. Schedule Weekly Calls (if needed)

For clients who require ongoing support or have more complex needs, itâ€™s essential to schedule weekly calls to maintain alignment and ensure that everything is progressing smoothly. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Ask for Client's Time Zone

Ask the client to provide their local time zone so you can align your call schedule accordingly.

Step 2: Set a Fixed Weekly Call Time and Day

Based on the client's time zone, establish a fixed time and day for the weekly calls to ensure both sides are aligned and can consistently meet. The fixed schedule helps maintain smooth communication.

Step 3: Create a Trello Task

After scheduling the weekly call, create a task in the Trello Dashboard for the meeting, including the scheduled time and key points to be discussed. This will ensure visibility and proper tracking for the management team.

Step 4: Inform the Management Team

After the meeting, make sure to update the Trello task with the outcomes of the meeting. This will ensure that the management team has visibility on the progress, and everyone stays aligned.
